Kingshot Player + Troops Capture v1

Fresh capture app focused only on player identity and troop preview. Records save by player_id. This version avoids calculator logic and only captures structured data.
How this version works
1) Upload a player identity screenshot.
2) Upload a troop preview screenshot.
3) Review parsed output.
4) Save by player_id.
5) Copy the database JSON when needed.
Player identity capture
Expected fields: player ID, player name, alliance, kingdom, power, kills.
Troop preview capture
Parses troop rows dynamically and normalizes them into infantry, cavalry, and archers.
Governor bonus overview capture
